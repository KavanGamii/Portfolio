Responsive Food Delivery Web Application

This project showcases a Responsive Food Delivery Web Application that brings convenience and health to the forefront of the digital dining experience. Designed with a modern aesthetic using HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript, and Bootstrap5, the application features:

  • User-Friendly Interface : An intuitive navigation system ensures that users can easily explore and order their favorite dishes.
  • Cross-Device Compatibility : The application’s responsive design guarantees a seamless experience across various devices, from mobile phones to desktops.

    • The web application’s clean design, highlighted by green accents, evokes a sense of freshness and aligns with the organic theme.

    Dynamic Dance Tournament Registration Portal

  • Engaging Content: Engage visitors with compelling text that tells the story of your tournament’s mission, vision, and the excitement of competitive dance.

    Deli//Break: Cake and Coffee Ordering Platform

    Indulge in a delightful culinary journey with Deli//Break, a sophisticated ordering platform for cakes, coffee, and pastries. Developed using HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and Bootstrap, this platform offers:

  • Diverse Selection: A wide array of delectable desserts and beverages, each presented with stunning visuals and an option to order instantly with a “Buy now” button.
  • Modern Aesthetics: A clean, contemporary design with neutral colors that highlight the products and create an inviting atmosphere for users.
  • Responsive Design: Ensures a seamless ordering experience across all devices, from mobile to desktop.

    • Deli//Break is not merely a platform; it’s a digital gastronomy exhibition that celebrates the love for patisserie and the passion for brewing.
